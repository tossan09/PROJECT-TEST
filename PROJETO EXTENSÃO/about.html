Takashi Yamamoto

CEO & Founder

Takashi Yamamoto founded RISE in 2010 with a vision to revolutionize the trucking industry in Japan. With over 20 years of experience in logistics and transportation, he has led the company to become one of the leading recruitment firms for truck drivers in the country. Under his leadership, RISE has established a reputation for excellence, reliability, and innovation in driver recruitment and placement.

Takashi is passionate about improving working conditions for truck drivers and is dedicated to matching skilled drivers with reputable companies. He believes in the power of continuous learning and development, and he ensures that RISE provides top-notch training programs for all its recruits.

When he is not steering the company towards new heights, Takashi enjoys long drives, exploring the countryside, and spending time with his family.
